Who We Are

Globalchem Manufacturing PLC is an Ethiopian import-export company providing secure and transparent trade solutions for manufacturing, agriculture, and trade sectors. It ensures smooth international transactions through collateral-backed financing, reliable forex access, and services like LC, TT, and CAD, helping businesses trade with confidence and security.

Our Mission

To deliver secure, transparent, and reliable import-export solutions that support businesses in manufacturing, agriculture, and trade. We ensure smooth transactions, timely payments, and consistent forex access, helping our clients operate confidently in global markets while promoting sustainable growth and long-term value.

Our Vision

To become a global industry leader, renowned for delivering unparalleled quality, transparency, and value in every transaction. We strive to foster secure, risk-free trade solutions that support our clients' success and contribute to the sustainable growth of global commerce.

How we work

At Globalchem Manufacturing PLC, we prioritize transparency, efficiency, and security. We understand our clients’ needs and provide tailored import-export solutions, using trusted financial tools like LC, TT, and CAD. With a strong network and reliable forex access, we ensure smooth, low-risk international transactions.
